
# Product Vision

## Objective
Digitize university operations and monetize via SaaS.

## Target Market
- African universities transitioning from manual systems

## Core Value
- Reduce paperwork
- Centralize academic data
- Automate grading & reporting

## MVP Scope
- Student lifecycle
- Enrollment
- Exams & GPA
- Document generation

## Monetization
- Tiered subscription
- Limits on:
  - number of students
  - storage
